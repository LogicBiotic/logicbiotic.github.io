Field & Lab Work: Trait-Based Ecology Research

As part of my BS Biology (Ecology and Evolutionary Biology Track) degree — graduated with Latin honors — I trained in scientific writing, research design, data wrangling, field collection, laboratory protocols, and data presentation with a focus on benthic macroinvertebrate ecology and limnology.

This work culminated in my undergraduate thesis, “Spatio-temporal Variations of the Functional Traits of Benthic Macroinvertebrates in the University of the Philippines Diliman Campus Waterways,” which combined the traditional taxonomic classification and scoring of identified macroinvertebrate families with the relatively new approach of analyzing their functional traits according to their genera using a fuzzy coding matrix. This allowed for the evaluation of freshwater ecosystem health across different months and seasons, as well as along the full stretch of waterways within the university.

Buwen: Python-Based Gacha Bot on Discord

A work-in-progress Discord bot that simulates daily gacha pulls using a custom database of collectible characters. Built in Python, Buwen is designed with modularity in mind, allowing for daily roll limits, rarity-based drop rates, and user info databasing. This ongoing personal project reflects my interest in game systems and backend logic, and is part of a larger goal to explore Discord API integration and my interest in game development.
