Outdoor Survival V1

A short text-based survival game built with HTML5, basic CSS, and JavaScript, this game is intended to be a proof of concept for a larger project with more features and variables involved.

In this game, you are suddenly dropped into the wild and must survive by managing your needs while constructing shelters and tools that increase your chances of being rescued.

A lot of features in this game are still in the works, such as a more complex crafting system, more items to find, and more random events to encounter. If you have any suggestions or feedback, please feel free to reach out!

Buwen: A Discord Gacha Bot

Buwen is a custom-built Discord gacha bot designed to capture the thrill of random rewards while integrating Filipino cultural elements. Players can roll daily to collect virtual cards of varying rarities and themes. Future updates aim to add upgrades to extend playtime, encourage frequent log-ins, and enhance the overall experience.

Developed with discord.py, Buwen features cooldown tracking, progress saving, and a constantly expanding pool of rewards. Options for secure and scalable user data management (such as Firebase or similar services) are being explored for future releases.
